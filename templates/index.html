📜 Server Rules

Violations may result in mute, kick, ban or permanent IP-ban.

§1 — General Conduct
  • ✦ Respect all players and staff at all times
  • ✦ No hate speech, racism, sexism or discrimination
  • ✦ No spamming or flooding any chat
  • ✦ No advertising other Minecraft servers
  • ✦ Keep global chat family-friendly and in English
§2 — Gameplay
  • ✦ No hacking, cheating or unauthorized client mods
  • ✦ No exploiting bugs — report them to staff immediately
  • ✦ No teaming in solo gamemodes
  • ✦ No stat boosting or account sharing
  • ✦ Allowed: Optifine, Sodium, Fabric API, Replay Mod
§3 — Store & Trading
  • ✦ No chargebacks — all purchases are final
  • ✦ No real-money trading outside the official store
  • ✦ No scamming players in trades
§4 — Punishments

Warning → Mute → Temp Ban → Permanent Ban
Hacking = Immediate Permanent Ban
Appeal via Support Tickets.
